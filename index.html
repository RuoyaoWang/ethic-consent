Participant Information

1. Research project title

Questionnaire about skills on crowdsourcing platform

2. Invitation paragraph

Before you decide whether or not to participate, it is important for you to understand why the research is being done and what it will involve. Please take time to read the following information carefully and discuss it with others if you wish. Ask us if there is anything that is not clear or if you would like more information. 3. What is the purpose of the preject?

The aim of the research is to build skill taxonomy on crowdsourcing platform by understanding what skills workers have; whether workers are proficient in these skills and their views on the task. The survey will be conducted for a few days. This is not for an educational qualification.

4. Why have I been chosen?

Participants will volunteer to participate in the survey. This survey will have 100 volunteers.

5. Do I have to take part?

It is up to you to decide whether or not to take part. If you do decide to take part you will be given this information sheet to keep (and be asked to sign a consent form). Because data have been anonymised and included within a large dataset, it will not be possible for a participantâ€™s data to be withdrawn from the research. You can withdraw from any on-going or future data collection before submit the questionnaire.

6. What will happen to me if I take part? What do I have to do?

Participants will complete a questionnaire. It takes about 15 minutes to complete this document. I will conduct a questionnaire survey of participants to understand the situation of workers looking for work on crowdsourcing platforms. The researcher wants to know what skills workers are looking for on a crowdsourcing platform; whether workers are proficient in these skills; and whether the worker is enjoy to finish the task. By collecting and analysing these data, it can effectively help the platform to establish a more reasonable skill taxonomy.

7. What are the possible disadvantages and risks of taking part?

Although the data will be anonymized, the possible risk of participation is the disclosure of personal data.

8. What are the possible benefits of taking part?

Although the people involved in the project have no direct interest, I hope that 100 volunteers will join the work.

9. Will my taking part in this project be kept confidential?

All the information that we collect about you during the course of the research will be kept strictly confidential and will only be accessible to members of the research team. You will not be able to be identified in any reports or publications unless you have given your explicit consent for this. If you agree to us sharing the information you provide with other researchers (e.g. by making it available in a data archive) then your personal details will not be included unless you explicitly request this.
